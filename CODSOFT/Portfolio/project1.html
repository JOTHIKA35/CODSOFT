Online Car rental system

A car rental is a vehicle that can be used temporarily for a fee for a certain period of time. The customer/user who needs a car should contact the rental company and rent a car. The staff of that car rental company also need to check their data to see whether the car is available or not for rental on that time. This online car rental system project is to book/reserve a car for a rent from anywhere where they are, so that customers do not need to call and spent unnecessary time to reserve a car. They can go online and reserve the car they want that are available. Customers and company will also able to track the car where it is. The project's goal is to automate car rental and reservation so that clients don't have to waste time calling and waiting for a vehicle. To convert the manual car rental procedure into a digital method.
